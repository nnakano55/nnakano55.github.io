Japanese Suicide: Why?

This project visualizes the japanese suicide rate throughout the years. We are specifically highlighting how the suicide rate can be fluctuated easily by these events. The story of the data is told through the progression of the data which would highlight the gradual/ubrupt change of suicide rates which show how suicide is deeply tied with Japan's social structure and tradition.
